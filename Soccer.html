All About Soccer

Soccer is a game involving two teams of 11 players who try to maneuver the ball into the other team's goal without using their hands or arms. Soccer is played by all age groups and all genders. Females as well as males between 3 and 90 enjoy playing soccer with their team mates. England is the first country to have invented soccer but now Brazil has taken the lead for the counrty that plays the most soccer.


Benefits

Soccer:
  • increases aerobic capacity and cardiovascular health
  • builds strength, flexibility and endurance
  • lowers body fat and improves muscle tone
  • increases muscle and bone strength
  • improves health due to shifts between walking, running and sprinting



    • Positions

    In soccer there are a variety of positions. There is goal keeper, striker, left winger, right winger, mid, centre mid, right mid, left mid, defence, centre back, right back and left back.The strikers and wingers are the ones who score the goals whereas the defence and backs are the ones who defend and stop the ball from coming towards their gaol. The mids are the ones who help defend when need be and help take the ball up to the strikers.
